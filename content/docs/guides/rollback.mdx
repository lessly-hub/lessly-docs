The short version: ask your agent `"Roll production back to the last known good deploy."` The agent calls [`lessly_list_deployments`](/docs/reference/tools/lessly_list_deployments) to find the candidate and [`lessly_rollback`](/docs/reference/tools/lessly_rollback) to promote it.
