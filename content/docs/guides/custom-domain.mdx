This page is a stub. The full how-to lands in the next docs cycle.

The short version: tell your agent `"Add example.com to my app."` The agent calls [`lessly_add_domain`](/docs/reference/tools/lessly_add_domain), shows you the DNS records to add at your registrar, then verifies once propagation completes.
